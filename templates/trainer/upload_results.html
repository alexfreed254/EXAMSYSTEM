TVET CDACC Computation

Weighted Scores:

  • Weighted = CA(60%) + SA(40%)
  • Overall = Theory(%) + Practical(%)

Competency Rules:

  • Theory ≥ 40%
  • Practical ≥ 50%
  • Overall ≥ 50%

Ratings:

  • 80-100: Attained Mastery
  • 65-79: Proficient
  • 50-64: Competent
  • <50: Not Yet Competent
